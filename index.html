About the Shop

Old-Fashioned Craft.
Downtown Abingdon.

Clifton's On Main is a neighborhood barbershop with real character. Situated in the heart of downtown Abingdon on Main Street, we offer more than just a haircut — it's a clean environment, real conversation, and a barber who takes his time to get it right.

Whether it's your first visit or you've been coming for years, expect the chair to be ready when your appointment is. No waiting around. No rushing through. Just good work done properly.
